🎨 Problem #1

Pretty ≠ performant.

Most designers focus on aesthetics, not ad psychology. They don't think about 3-second hook rates, scroll-stops, or what actually drives a click-through.

Creative fatigue kills momentum.

If you're not testing fresh angles every week, your CPA climbs until the account becomes unscalable. Most brands run out of ideas before they run out of budget.

Slow teams lose ground fast.

By the time one revision cycle ends, the algorithm has moved on. Speed and creative quality can't be a tradeoff — you need both, consistently.
